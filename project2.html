SuperStore Sales Dashboard Analysis

📊 SuperStore Sales Dashboard Analysis

Author: Deepak Annapureddy

Tools Used: Power BI, DAX, Excel, SQL

🔍 Project Overview

  • Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance across regions, categories, and customer segments.
  • Implemented KPIs and forecasting to support data-driven business decisions.
  • Visualized trends in sales, profit, and customer behavior using dynamic filters and charts.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Analyze sales and profit trends over time
  • Identify top-performing products and categories
  • Perform region-wise and state-wise sales analysis
  • Forecast future sales for 15 days
  • Create interactive and user-friendly dashboard

📊 Sample Dashboard Visuals

🧠 Key DAX Measures

  • Total Sales = SUM(Orders[Sales])
  • Total Profit = SUM(Orders[Profit])
  • Total Orders = DISTINCTCOUNT(Orders[Order ID])
  • Profit Margin % = DIVIDE(SUM(Orders[Profit]), SUM(Orders[Sales]), 0)
  • Sales Growth % = (Current - Previous) / Previous

📈 Insights Gained

  • California generates the highest sales revenue
  • Office Supplies is the top-performing category
  • Phones and Chairs contribute major revenue
  • Standard Class is the most used shipping mode
  • Sales show an overall upward trend
  • Forecast indicates continued growth in upcoming days
